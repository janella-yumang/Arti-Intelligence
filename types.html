Types of AI

Narrow AI (Weak AI)

Narrow AI is built for one specific task. Examples include recommendation engines, face recognition, language translation, and spam filtering. This is the form of AI widely used today.

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)

AGI is a hypothetical AI that could perform any intellectual task at a human level across different fields, not just one specialized task.

Artificial Super Intelligence (ASI)

ASI is a theoretical concept where AI surpasses human intelligence in almost every domain. It is still speculative and raises serious ethical and safety questions.

Comparison Snapshot

What exists now?

  • Narrow AI is real and already deployed in many industries.
  • AGI and ASI are not yet achieved.
  • Research continues to improve capability and safety.

Why this matters

  • Helps set realistic expectations of current AI.
  • Supports better policy and education planning.
  • Encourages ethical design before systems become more powerful.
